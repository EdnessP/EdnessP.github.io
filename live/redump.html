Redump Tools

Tools related to working with data on Redump.org.

Physical Format Information & Security Sector Headers

Dumped and retrieved from DVD, UMD, and XGD format discs.

Parses the Physical Format Information (PFI) and Security Sector (SS) header hexdumps retrieved from DVD, UMD, and XGD discs.

JS (Live).


Permanent Information & Control

Dumped and retrieved from Blu-Ray discs.

Parses the Permanent Information & Control (PIC) hexdump retrieved from pressed BD-ROM discs.

Py (Standalone), JS (Live).


PlayStation 3 Disc Key & Disc ID

Dumped and retrieved from PlayStation 3 discs.

Converts the PlayStation 3 Data1 key to and from the Disc Key used for decrypting game disc sectors, and the Data2 key to and from the Disc ID.

JS (Live).

Disc Key
Data2
